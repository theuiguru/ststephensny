→ OFFICIAL TOURNAMENT SCHEDULE (*One Game Elimination)

  • Registration: 9:00AM-9:45AM - Sign in and receive team t-shirts, shoes, and lane assignments
  • Inauguration Ceremony: 9:45AM-10:00AM
  • Preliminary Round: 10:00AM-12:00PM (2 hours of open bowling)
    • Each team plays two games, take the average score
    • Eight teams with the top 8 average scores move on to playoffs
  • Lunch Break: 12:00PM-1:00PM - 1 pizza pie, 1 pitcher soda per lane
  • *Quarterfinals: 1:00PM-2:00PM - Eight teams, #1 seed vs #8 seed, #2 vs #7 seed, etc., winners move on
  • *Semifinals: 2:00PM-3:00PM - Four teams, teams play head-to-head, winners move on
  • *Finals: 3:00PM-4:00PM - Two teams, head-to-head, winner is champion
  • Raffle Drawing: 4:00PM - Winning raffle tickets are drawn, prizes awarded

→ SCHOLARSHIP DETAILS

  • ALL the proceeds for this event will be going towards the Mar Makarios Scholarship Fund. This Scholarship Fund was set up in the memory of the Late His Grace Dr. Thomas Mar Makarios.

  • The Scholarship will have three components:
    • 1. Two Sunday school students who score first and second ranks in the 12th grade centralized examination from the American Diocese.
    • 2. Two Sunday school students from our parish (St. Stephens Orthodox Church) who score the first and second ranks in the 12th grade centralized examination.
    • 3. To finance the education expense of needy children in India.
